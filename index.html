About Zeffa

The brand Zeffa Clothing has been inspired by the laid back lifestyle of the surf culture. With Zeffa being based on the coast, we have embraced the sea loving community by designing clothing that is not only comfortable and stylish but represents what we have grown to love in our mellow hometown of Ballito. Zeffa is here to combine courage with comfort by giving you a new look on life as well as in the mirror. Apart from being your new favourite merch, Zeffa intends to make an impact in areas of South Africa where it is greatly needed, helping various wildlife foundations and charities of our beautiful country we call home. Keep spreading the stoke! The Zeffa crew welcomes you!
